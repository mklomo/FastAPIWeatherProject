Weather Service: A RESTful weather service



The weather service.


Endpoints

  • Current weather in a city

    GET /api/weather/{city}?country={country}&state={state}

    Parameters

    • Required: city={city} - the city you want to get the weather
    • Optional: state={state} - the state you want to get the weather (US only, two letter abbr)
    • Optional: country={country} - the country you want to get the weather - US if none specific (Two letter abbr)
    • Optional: units={units} - the units you want to get the weather {metric, imperial, standard}
